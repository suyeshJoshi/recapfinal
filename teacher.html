For Teachers

Control over content and time spent at home by students – When your distilled knowledge is published, you not only reinforce what has been taught,students save time in creating their own version of notes and clearly helping them with what to focus on and how to explain things. You can also explain things in advance of discussion / revision in the classroom.

Comprehensive learning guide that progresses all year – You can publish the Units as soon as you finish teaching them or flip it and give it to them in advance to come prepared, making the learning experience different. This way the Learning Units keep pace with what’s completed and saves time all around.

Increased attention span and higher probability of absorption and retention – Since the Learning Units are of short duration and a mix of audio, images, videos or animation etc the chances of learning increases as all learning theories agree.

Step by step explanation for enhanced comprehension – Wherever steps are involved you can use the Progressive mode of creation where each step can be explained and the students can flip between steps to get a perfect understanding.

Increase reach – If you are an independent Tuition Teacher or Trainer, you can use this platform to reach students who may not be able to physically come to your location. Let them access your content virtually.
